FAQ:

What do we gain by reporting and tracking issues?

  • At Illinois Tech we do love and care.

  • Our campus is like a beautiful woman.
    When you do keep her clean,
    she forgets she is beautiful.

Is any action guaranteed to fix the reported issues?
  • We hope to get involved in taking action as well.

What is the benefit?
  • Remember Wikipedia? Google?

  • Now imagine something like that for our campus civic issues

  • Documenting issues is the first step towards solving them.

How do I benefit, individually?
  • If you are into such things, we do have Scoreboards.

What kind of issues can be reported?
  • Roads
  • Water and Sanitation
  • Electricity
  • Garbage
  • Public Transport
  • Traffic
  • Trees
  • Parks and Playgrounds
  • Campus shops

How do I report an issue?
  1. Message us on Facebook
  2. Tweet to us on @Open IIT-C Issues
  3. Share and mention us on Instagram @OpenIIT-CIssues
